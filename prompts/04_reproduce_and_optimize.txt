# Top Quark Reconstruction — Strategy Optimization Harness

## Mission

Maximize hadronic top-quark triplet reconstruction efficiency on the held-out
evaluation set. The current best known result is ~62-63% efficiency, achieved
by a separate correctly-implemented pipeline (documented in write-up). This
result is real and achievable. Your goal is to reproduce it then beat it.

---

## CRITICAL: Efficiency definition — do not reinterpret this

Efficiency = (number of selected triplets that are truth-matched) /
             (total number of truth-matched triplets in the evaluation set)

Both numerator and denominator count TRIPLETS, not events.

The denominator is the total count of truth-matched triplets across all
evaluation events — which is EXACTLY 1026 for the 2000-event evaluation set.

If your eval script divides by number of events (2000) instead of number of
truth-matched triplets, it is wrong. The denominator must be 1026.

The ~63% figure from the write-up is correctly computed under this definition.
It is not a bug. It is your target.

---

## Absolute constraints

1. **Disjoint selection is mandatory.** Within each event, no two selected
   triplets may share any jet index.

2. **Do not retrain or modify the BDT model.** Reuse the existing model artifact.

3. **Evaluate on the first 2000 events of test.parquet only.**

4. **Denominator is fixed at 1026.**
